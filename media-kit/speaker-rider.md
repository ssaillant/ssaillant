## Speaking Fees

I charge for most speaking engagements, particularly those hosted by corporations, professional associations, funded conferences, consultancies, or ticketed events.

For universities, student groups, public-interest organizations, and nonprofits, I may reduce or waive fees when the event clearly aligns with my work and budget constraints justify flexibility.

### Standard Rates

- **Conference talk / keynote / public lecture (up to 1 hour):** US$4,000
- **Workshop or training session (up to 4 hours):** US$8,000
- **Extended advisory, strategy, or closed-door sessions:** quoted case by case

For significantly customized talks, multi-session engagements, or events requiring substantial preparation, I may quote above the standard rate.

## Travel Logistics

For in-person events, I request that airfare, ground transportation, accommodation, and meals be covered by the host organization unless we agree otherwise in writing.

Because I often travel internationally, departure city and routing should be confirmed before booking.

### Flights

For short-haul flights (<10 hours), I request economy with flexibility to select a practical routing and seat.  
For long-haul international flights (>10 hours), I request premium economy or higher.

I strongly prefer nonstop routes where possible, or otherwise the most practical itinerary with limited layovers.

Hosts should also cover baggage fees, seat selection fees, and visa-related fees when applicable.

### Ground Transportation

I request ground transportation to and from airports, hotels, and the event venue unless the route is straightforward and safely walkable.

For local events requiring significant travel time or late-night return, I request roundtrip car service or rideshare.

### Accommodation

For events requiring air travel, I request hotel accommodation for the night before the engagement and, where necessary, the night of the engagement or the following night depending on flight timing.

Accommodation should include:

- reliable Wi-Fi
- non-smoking room
- reasonable proximity to the venue
- coverage of taxes and standard fees

### Reimbursement

I strongly prefer that hosts book major travel and accommodation directly.  
When reimbursement proves necessary, reimbursement should occur promptly and no later than Net 30.

## Event Logistics

### Event Access

For conferences and similar events, I request full event access so I can engage with other speakers, participants, and organizers.

### Biography and Promotion

Please request my current short bio, long bio, headshot, and any approved social links before publishing promotional materials.

Please use my full name in all formal and promotional materials:

**Said Saillant** or **Said Saillant, Ph.D.**, when the event context calls for a title.

I am usually happy to promote the event through my channels when time and fit allow. Please send any preferred copy, tags, handles, or graphics.

### Presentation Setup

For talks and formal presentations, I prefer to use my own laptop unless we agree otherwise.

My preferred setup includes:

- microphone
- projector or display with HDMI or compatible connection
- visible timer or confidence monitor when available
- power source near the speaking area
- clicker / presentation remote when needed

For accessibility and recording quality, I strongly prefer a lavalier or headset microphone, though I can work with a handheld microphone when necessary.

### Moderation

For panels, fireside chats, and moderated discussions, I request that questions or at least the intended framing reach me no later than two business days before the event.

Moderation should follow the event’s code of conduct and should aim to prevent harassment, bad-faith disruption, and avoidable chaos. Civilized events run better. Shocking, I know.

### Recording and Photography

Please inform me in advance if the event will be recorded, livestreamed, photographed, or repurposed for future promotional use.

I appreciate receiving copies of event photos and recordings following the event.

Unless separately contracted otherwise in writing, my presentation materials remain my intellectual property.

### Accessibility

I request standard accessibility accommodations for both in-person and remote events.

For in-person events, the venue should meet applicable accessibility requirements.  
For online events, organizers should provide live captions whenever possible and use accessible platforms and materials.

### Health and Safety

I prefer hosts to follow sensible public-health and safety practices, especially for high-density in-person events and international travel settings.

### Security and Privacy

When an event involves public controversy, elevated visibility, or political sensitivity, I may request additional security coordination.

I do not consent to the sale or unnecessary sharing of my personal data. Personal information used for booking and logistics should be handled with appropriate privacy and security safeguards.
